# Class Attendance App User Guide

## Overview
The Class Attendance App is a mobile application designed to streamline attendance tracking for educational institutions. It supports both teachers and students with features for session management, QR code scanning, analytics, and more. The app includes offline capabilities, notifications, and privacy controls.

## For Teachers

### Signing Up
1. Download the app from the App Store or Google Play.
2. Open the app and select "Sign Up" as a Teacher.
3. Enter your email, create a password, and provide institution details.
4. Verify your email to complete registration.

### Creating Sessions
1. Log in to your teacher account.
2. Navigate to the "Create Session" screen.
3. Enter session details: class name, date, time, and duration.
4. Generate a unique QR code for the session.
5. Share the QR code with students or display it in class.

### Viewing Attendance
1. Go to the "Session Attendance" screen.
2. Select a past or ongoing session.
3. View the list of students who have checked in.
4. Mark attendance manually if needed for absentees.

### Analytics
1. Access the "Analytics Dashboard" from the teacher home screen.
2. View charts and reports on attendance rates, trends, and student participation.
3. Filter by class, date range, or individual students.
4. Export reports for administrative purposes.

## For Students

### Signing Up
1. Download the app from the App Store or Google Play.
2. Select "Sign Up" as a Student.
3. Provide your email, student ID, and create a password.
4. Verify your email to activate your account.

### Scanning QR Code
1. Log in to your student account.
2. Open the "Scan QR" screen.
3. Point your camera at the teacher's QR code displayed in class.
4. The app will automatically record your attendance for the session.
5. Receive confirmation of successful check-in.

### Viewing History
1. Navigate to the "Attendance History" screen.
2. Browse your past attendance records by session or date.
3. View details like check-in time and session information.
4. Track your attendance percentage over time.

## Key Features

### Offline Mode
- The app functions without an internet connection for core features like QR scanning and viewing history.
- Attendance data is stored locally and synced when connectivity is restored.
- Ideal for areas with poor network coverage.

### Notifications
- Receive push notifications for upcoming sessions, attendance reminders, and updates.
- Teachers get alerts for low attendance rates or session changes.
- Students are notified of new sessions or missed check-ins.

### Privacy Settings
- Control data sharing in the "Privacy Settings" screen.
- Opt-in or out of analytics sharing, location tracking, and notifications.
- All personal data is encrypted and stored securely on Firebase.
- Comply with data protection regulations like GDPR.
